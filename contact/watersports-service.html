Float tubes rental for all the family.

In this lightweight floating tube, single person watercraft that enables you to reach wilderness streams, ponds and lakes. Usually where there is no boat access, the fun is letting the current pull you while floating on the tube or take in the sport of fishing, that has been loved by many over the past centuries.

Get into the fun as hopped in the float tube as float on the water without worrying if you are having trouble swimming. Just sit or lie down on your float tubes as you relax floating in the ocean as you make a stop on your island hopping tour with your friends and family.

Let your family and kids enjoy the water as they float around with our float tubes and dont let them miss the fun under the sun. Floating on tubes can be enjoyed by any age and float tubes will be your guide on learning how to swim.

Towed Floating tubes rental.

About Scotty's Action Sport Network watersports and SUBA diving.

Where is Scotty’s Action Sports Network?
How can I make a booking?
You may call us direct or simply use our Contact form.
Can I make a booking on the day of activity?
Yes, you can. But sometimes we cannot accommodate your preferred time.
We strongly recommend you to make advance booking.
There are no cancellation fees.
Can I change my booking on the day of activity?
Yes. Please check your booking before departure
Can I pay by card?
Yes (VISA, MasterCard, American Express, JCB). If you are staying at Shangri-La, Hilton, or Plantation bay Resort and the activity is done within the resort, you may charge it to your room as well.
Should I wear swim suit before going to the Sea sports center?
Yes, but you can change in our restroom.
Can I join alone?
It depends on the activities. Island excursions and tube rides are minimum of two persons. If there are no other customers you have to pay for two. Other activities such as scuba diving, parasailing, tow sports are minimum of one person only.
Scuba Diving
What should I bring?
If you are a licensed diver, please bring a Certification card, log books and some of your own dive equipment.

If you are not licensed, just wear swim suit and come down to the center.
Never tried before can I enjoy?
Yes you can. You can try discover scuba diving (DSD) or open water diver licenses course.
I am wearing contact lenses or wearing eye glasses.
We have some prescription masks. But there are limited. We recommend to book it for advance.
I have a license but my friend doesn’t. Can we dive together?
Yes you can. You may join in while your friend does the pool session training or you can wait for the actual ocean dive, alternatively you can book for an exclusive boat for an extra charge.
Can my family ride a boat with me and other divers?
Yes they can. And you may rent our snorkeling set, alternatively you can book for an exclusive boat for an extra charge.
Parasailing
Can we get wet?
Yes you can. But if you don’t want to get wet, please let us know. We can cancel touch and go.
Can I bring camera up in the air?
Yes you can but if it's not water proof we recommend to leave it on land. We can take pictures for you from the boat and save them on a CD for 500 pesos.
What is touch and go?
While you are flying you go down slowly touching the water at about waist level.
It is very exciting.
Can I ride together with a friend?
Yes you can.
Island Excursions
What should I bring?
The tour includes life jacket, snorkel set, fins, towel. We recommend to bring you own sun protection and camera.
Can I hire the boat only for my family?
Yes, you can. You can have an exclusive boat for an extra charge.
How long to get to the island?
These are from the hotel to the island, it depends on the weather condition.
  1. Nalusuan - 40 minutes
  2. Stilt restaurant - 40 minutes
  3. Pandanon Island - 50 minutes
  4. Nalusuan island to pandanon island - 40 minutes
  5. Bohol Island - 1 hour.
Tow Sports
What should I bring?
We have life jacket and equipment. Just wear a swim suit and bring sun protection.
It's my first time to try.
No need to worry. We will teach you how to enjoy the activity.
Can you take pictures?
Yes we can. We can take pictures for you, from the boat and save them on a CD for 500 pesos.
