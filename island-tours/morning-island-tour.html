Sanctuary Morning island Tour

Just northern east coast of Mactan Island, Cebu, the Shangri-La Marine Sanctuary is one of the best dive sites in Cebu and a never to be missed experience as you will be able to see a stunning marine sanctuary and the abundant and healthy corals extends its way out to the steeper slopes. Just for PHP1,600 / $32 per person, you can take part of this boat Snorkeling Tour and dive into a sanctuary that is just within the resort. A minimum of 2 persons are required for this package and an approximation of an hour for this activity.

  • Boat Snorkeling Tour.
  • Shangri-la Marine Sanctuary.
  • Duration: approx 1 hour.
  • Price: 1,600 PHP / $32 per person.
  • Minimum of 2 persons.
