Morning island hopping tours and snorkeling tours.

Take our morning island snorkeling tour and dive into the wonders of the underwater gardens of Nalusuan island and be mesmerized by the vibrant colors of their marine sanctuary and mingle with the underwater residents of the area. As the sun rises, the colors underneath will shine and it will feels like you are looking through a colorful kaleidoscope.

Nalusuan morning island tour.

Take your morning island tour with our snorkeling adventure to Nalusuan island! Snorkel into the wonders of underwater garden of this island marine sanctuary and be dazzle by the vibrant colors reflected by the rising sun as you start your day snorkeling in the middle of the marine life during this tour.

Enjoy this island snorkeling tour for 3 to 4 hours at just PHP2,700 / $54 per person and a minimum of 2 persons in order to take part of this morning boat and snorkeling tour adventure.

Sanctuary morning island tour.

Just northern east coast of Mactan island, Cebu, the Shangri-La marine sanctuary is one of the best dive sites and snorkeling site in Cebu and a never to be missed experience, as you will be able to see a stunning marine sanctuary abundant and healthy corals extending its way out to the steeper slopes in this island tour.

Just for PHP1,600 / $32 per person, you can take part of this morning island snorkeling tour and dive with a snorkel gear into a sanctuary that is just within the resort. A minimum of 2 persons are required for this island snorkeling tour package with an approximation of a duration of an hour.

    Morning island boat snorkeling tour.

    Shangri-la marine sanctuary.

  • Duration: approx 1 hour.
  • Price: 1,600 PHP / $32 per person.
  • Minimum of 2 persons.
