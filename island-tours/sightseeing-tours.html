Sight seeing tours

Indulge your thirst for adventure as Scotty’s Action Sports Network offers a Sight Seeing Adventure to any islands you desire within Mactan and all around Olango Islands and you could choose either you will take it with a speedboat or by local banca for better sight seeing.

Relaxing boat tour of the tropical waters surrounding Boracay. Get to view the long white beach and some tropical islands with its local vilages and some fishermen and greet other tourist taking their tour around Boracay while your own our leisurely boat cruise.
