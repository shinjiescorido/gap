With our special high range parasail boat (up to 350 meters / +1,000 feet), marvel your way through the horizon, both on land and on sea and on this intense moment, you will realize just how small you are in comparison to the world we live on.

Enjoy this experience as you fly on a parasail through the sky and gaze into the vast wonders of the world as it unfolds itself in front of you. Never miss this opportunity as you could witness a dreamy world up there and let the sun eclipse you in this brave parasailing adventure that you will forever relive. Gleam into a birds-eye view and let parasail wings take you up so high or take a dip and splash your way as you kiss the sea floor while racing with the wind. Make your choice as we will make this to the sky experience come true to you. Just dont miss our parasail package!
