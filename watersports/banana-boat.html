Banana Boat Adventures

Bring over your family and friends on this fun ride and let everyone hold on as much as possible as the speedboat runs it way through the gushing waves and it might flip away the inflatable boat, and you’d be really lucky to hold on. Going on a Banana Boat Ride is truly a great way to take a break from your everyday routine as it is exhilarating, fun and very safe. The fun and excitement can be enjoyed by your friends and family as well as the young ones and let this ride be their starting adventure and make this an unforgettable vacation ever.

Get into the action and fun with our Banana Boat Ride and feel the thrill as you get yourself into a wild ride. Hold on tight as the driver throws everyone aboard in the deep waters. One of the most popular and fun water activity that everyone in the family could enjoy. One of the greatest group activity you could have as the speed rushes with the waves while you are holding on and the fun just keeps on going. Bring in your family and friends get carried away to the fun and excitement the Banana Boat Ride provides. Hop in and hold on tight as you don’t want to get left behind in this exciting fun ride.

Mactan Cebu Banana Boat Ride Prices

For as low as PHP 1,350 / $27 per person, a good 15 minute ride could tire you out of fun but if you want to take it further, PHP 2,250 / $45 per person package is good for 30 minutes of this exhilarating ride for you and for your family. A minimum of 2 person is needed as we don’t want you to be alone in this ride. Don’t miss out the fun.
