Kneeboarding: Easy and enjoyable watersports activity.

Come and learn kneeboarding basics and progress to freestyle knee-boarding.

Knee boarding complete gears available for rent at Scotty's Action Sports Network.

Kneeboarding and extreme watersports.

Hold on tight and dont let it take you down as you go on the rushing waves while being pulled with intense speed as you balance on your knees on kneeboarding.

Jump on waves and glide up in the air with a kneeboard and let the adrenaline rush through as you go over in this extreme watersport (kneeboarding) and fully immersed in the sport and lifestyle and can be enjoyed by any age as we provide safety measures on this extreme ride without taking the fun away.

Kneeboarding is a watersport that is excellent to start in a boat-towed water sport on which you can get up on your board easier because of low center for gravity. So get in your kneeboarding gear and dont let the waves pull you down in this kneeboard extreme ride.
