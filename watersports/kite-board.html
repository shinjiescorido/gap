Kiteboarding: Learn how to practice the kitesurf extreme watersport.

From beginner to advanced. Experience kitesurfing now at Scotty's Boracay.

The best places to practice kitesurf and kiteboard.

Kiteboarding and kitesurfing extreme watersports.

Kite surfing is a lot more easier than you can think, but it ask for focus, which is a rewarding sport. Take your water sport skills on surfing, wakeboarding and kite flying as you will get to experience those in one and take in the freedom and feel the supreme energy of the wind.

One of the best beach paradise, Boracay is also known as the best kite boarding - kitesurfing spot in Asia! It is a great place for beginners to learn kite surf, and for experienced riders to show off their tricks and ride the waves on a new level. Get into your gear and take control of the wind as you cruise along the surface of the water with a kitesurf!

Scotty’s Action Sports Network also offers several kitesurf courses available for you if you want to get into the fun.

Boracay kiteboarding and kitesurfing prices.

No worries for newbies as SASN (Scotty's Action Sport Network) offers introductory kite boarding or kitesurfing course which includes short lectures for 30 minutes and get the feel of kite surfing for an hour and a half on land first before emerging on the waves. This kitesurf course is for PHP 5,000 / $100 per person and a total 2 hours session.

Take a kite boarding / kitesurfing 1 day course and learn all the basic skills and knowledge of Kite boarding with our professional kite surfer instructors. Get to learn water kite control, land and launching in water, kite surf rescue in water and pack down with this 3 hours session for just PHP 9,500 / $190 per person.

Learn kite boarding and kitesurf 2 days course all the basic of safety, the kite setup, flying and control, body drag and you will take your learning onboard in the water! This kitesurf course is good for 2 days with 3 hours sessions each day at PHP 19,000 / $380 per person.

Get into the fun as you take the kite boarding or kite surfing 3 days course where you will be guided step by step on how to ride with the wind, change direction and become an independent kiter. Ride with the waves in this 3 day course with 3 hours per session for just PHP 28,000 / $560 per person.

Cant get enough of kite boarding or kite surfing? Get the kite boarding or kite surfing advance course as your prior experience will be put into the challenge as you will learn how to ride upwind, take a jump and do tricks with the waves. In this kitesurf course, the lessons are good for 5 hours for just PHP 17,000 / $340 per person. You will be needing to supply your own gear for this course. If you want to rent our kitesurf gears, we can provide you a special prices for this course.

Get your own kiteboarding or kitesurfing private coach and get in-depth guide for a very personalized kitesurf lesson and get into the waves as soon as you learn the basic principles of kite surfing. Private coaching last for 1 hour and it is already included with gears good for PHP 5,000 / $100 per person. You will be needing to supply your own gear for this course. If you want to rent our kitesurf gears, we can provide you special prices for this course.
