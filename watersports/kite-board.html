Kiteboarding: Learn this complex but very rewarding extreme Watersport

From beginner to advance. Experience kiteboarding now at Scotty's

Learn freestyle kiteboarding

Kiteboarding Extreme Watersports

Kite Surfing is a complex, but rewarding sport. Take your water sport skills on surfing, wakeboarding and kite flying as you will get to experience those in one and take in the freedom and feel the supreme energy of the win
One of the best beach paradise, Boracay is also known as the Best Kite Boarding-spot in Asia! It is a great place for experienced riders to show off their tricks and ride the waves on a new level. Get into your gear and take control of the wind as you cruise along the surface of the water! Scotty’s Action Sports Network also offers several courses available for you if you want to get into the fun.

Boracay Kiteboarding Prices

No worries for newbies as SANS offers Introductory Course which includes short lectures for 30 minutes and get the feel of kite for an hour and a half on land first before emerging on the waves. This course is for PHP 5,000 / $100 per person and a total 2 hours session.
Take a Kite Boarding 1 Day Course and learn all the basic skills and knowledge of Kite Boarding with our professional instructors. Get to learn water kite control, land and launching in water, kite rescue in water and pack down with this 3 hours session for just PHP 9,500 / $190 per person.
Learn Kite Boarding 2 Day Course all the basic of safety, kite setup, flying and control, body drag and you will take your learning onboard in the water! This course is good for 2 days with 3 hours sessions each day at PHP 19,000 / $380 per person.
Get into the fun as you take the Kite Boarding 3 Day Course where you will be guided step by step on how to ride with the wind, change direction and become an independent kiter. Ride with the waves in this 3 day course with 3 hours per session for just PHP 28,000 / $560 per person.
Cant get enough of Kite Boarding? Get the Kite Boarding Advance Course as your prior experience will be put into the challenge as you will learn how to ride upwind, take a jump and do tricks with the waves. In this course, the lessons are good for 5 hours for just PHP 17,000 / $340 per person. You will be needing to supply your own gear for this course. If you want to rent our gears, we can provide you special prices for this course.
Get your own Kite Boarding Private Coach and get in-depth guide for a very personalized lesson and get into the waves as soon as you learn the basic principles of Kite Boarding. Private Coaching last for 1 hour and it is already included with gears good for PHP 5,000 / $100 per person. You will be needing to supply your own gear for this course. If you want to rent our gears, we can provide you special prices for this course.
