Dive into the tropical island of Moalboal and its rich marine life, coral reefs and deep walls.

Moalboal SCUBA diving.

Moalboal is known for its famous sardine run and it is a scuba divers paradise as it has lots of diving spots and marine sanctuaries that stretch out within its area. Witness a massive school of sardines as they circle around the warm tropical waters of Moalboal.

    Moalboal SCUBA diving.

  • Duration: all day.
