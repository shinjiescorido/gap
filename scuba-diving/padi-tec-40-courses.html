PADI Tec 40 course

Transition from recreational SCUBA diving to technical diving and build up your knowledge and skills as you will get to gain experience in this extreme adventure. Take our tec 40 course and take your first step into the world of technical diving. You must be an advanced open water diver with enriched air diver certification and at least 10 dives using nitrox deeper than 18 meters and certified deep diver with atleast 10 dives down to 30 meters. This course is good for 4 days which includes your diving gears.
