Shangri-la Mactan resort and spa.

One of the known 5-star resort in Mactan and Cebu, and once the Philippines’ largest resort, with 13 hectares and equipped with 530 guest rooms and suites, own a private man-made beach cove, 2 outdoor swimming pools, a 6-hole golf course, a marine sanctuary covering 6 hectares that holds over 100 species of fish and corals, as well as two sunken ships placed there to form artificial coral for the fishes.

Just a 15-minute drive from Mactan-Cebu international airport, you will be assisted by the helpful staff to book and organise your watersports, island tours, snorkeling and scuba diving activities in Lapu-Lapu City. Shangri-la Mactan provides trendy rooms with a private balcony, a spa bath with views of the sea and the garden, rooms fully air conditioned with a mini bar, gym with a swimming pool and saunas and jacuzzi, and wide selections of restaurants with wifi access throughout the facilities.

Shangri-la Mactan also offers one of the most fascinating underwater world as it has a diversified marine sanctuary and ocean playground where you could do snorkeling, scuba diving, extreme watersports, island hopping and tour, and any beach activities that you like. These featured activities can be booked to Scotty’s action sports network where they will provide the proper equipments and safety measures for you fun and exciting adventures whether on the pool, confined water, above the surface and even under the sea. Don’t miss Shangri-la’s marine sanctuary and its blooming underwater world with your friends and family.

This marine sanctuary is maintained alongside with the Amores charities Inc, Scotty’s action sports network and the government of Lapu-Lapu city.

Shangri-la Boracay.

A private paradise surrounded by natural beauty, that is Shangri-la’s Boracay resort and spa. A breathtaking resort with its crystal clear blue waters viewed from the outside and the white sand stretching on each side. With its luxurious rooms and high-end amenities in a secluded spot amid lush tropical gardens in northern Boracay, Shangri-la Boracay resort and spa is known for its 5-star resort offering stylish rooms, private pools or outdoor whirlpools, spa service, butler service and a tons of activities for all ages.
